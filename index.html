3 AI Automations That Run Your Business For You

You finish the job. The system does everything else.

The Chaser — Stacks Your Google Reviews

Most tradespeople do great work but have 3 reviews on Google because they forget to ask. Or they ask once, the customer means to do it, and never gets round to it.

The Chaser fixes that permanently.

  • Automatically texts the customer asking for a review — up to 4 times over a few weeks.
  • Smart timing. Each follow-up is spaced out so it doesn't feel pushy.
  • The moment they leave a review, it stops immediately. No annoying extra messages.
  • You never have to remember to ask, chase, or send a link. It just happens.

The result: Clients who use this consistently go from a handful of reviews to 30, 50, 100+ — and Google rewards that with higher rankings in local search.

🛡️

The Filter — Protects You From Bad Public Reviews

Here's the problem with just handing out a Google review link: if someone's unhappy, they leave a 1-star review and the whole world sees it. You can't delete it. It sits there killing your credibility.

The Filter makes that almost impossible.

  • When a customer taps the review link, they're first asked to rate you out of 5.
  • 4 or 5 stars? They're sent straight to your Google profile to leave the review publicly.
  • 3 stars or below? They're redirected to a private feedback form instead. The review never touches Google.
  • You get notified privately, so you can reach out and fix the issue before it becomes a public problem.

The result: Your Google profile only shows your best work. Unhappy customers feel heard. Problems get solved quietly instead of publicly.

🔄

The Multiplier — Turns Past Customers Into New Ones

You do a great job for someone, they're happy, and then... nothing. Six months later they need more work done and can't remember your name. Or their neighbour asks "know a good plumber?" and they can't find your number.

  • Automatically texts past customers a branded referral page with a discount offer — every few months, on a schedule you set.

  • Drives repeat business from people who've already used you and trust you.
  • Generates new referrals from their network — the highest-converting leads you can get.

The result: Instead of relying on word-of-mouth and hoping people remember you, the system does the reminding for you. Repeat jobs and referrals on autopilot.
